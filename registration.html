REGISTRATION

Register early because space is limited to the first 200 applicants! Achievements Unlimited camp is held annually in July for one week. Campers must attend a mandatory orientation at CAROLINA COURTS INDIAN TRAIL, CHARLOTTE, NC - 5:00 PM, Monday, July 20th. All interested applicants are encouraged to APPLY EARLY because space is limited. Achievements Unlimited Basketball School takes great pride in providing personalized attention to each camper. As such, admission is strictly limited to the first 200 applicants. Each camper will participate in a group with his/her own age, height, and ability level. Location: CAROLINA COURTS INDIAN TRAIL, CHARLOTTE, NC
