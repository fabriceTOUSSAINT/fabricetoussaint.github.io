THE GAME

FORMAT

18 Hole Modified Scramble

Each team will consist of one (1) foursome and one (1) celebrity golfer. A Modified Scramble Best Net Ball scoring system will be used.

Modified Scramble Best Net Ball.

  • Everyone will tee off from official men’s or women’s tee box.
  • The team captain will select which drive will used for play.
  • Each player’s drive must be used at least two (2) times during the round.
  • All players must place their ball within one club length of the selected tee shot.
  • Each team member will play their own ball from there until the ball is holed.
  • The “no hunt rule” is in effect.

Ties are determined by a scorecard playoff starting with the #1 handicap hole.

Championship Shoot-Out

The top 4 teams from the modified scramble will participate in an additional 3-hole alternating shot format shoot-out to determine the First, Second, Third and Fourth place winners of the 2013 HoopTee Celebrity Golf Classic.

  • Each team will play holes #1, 17 and 18.
  • The team captain will determine the order of play for their respective teams. The teams must follow this order until all four holes are complete. Any deviation will result in disqualification.
  • The final results will be based on total gross score.
  • Ties will be determined by a scorecard playoff starting with the #1 handicap hole of 1, 17, 18. Additional ties will be determined by a one hole sudden death on #18.

LOCATION

The Golf Club at Ballantyne's championship golf course is situated on a dynamic terrain, carefully crafted into the natural hills of the Piedmont. Golf enthusiasts of all skill levels will enjoy the challenging but fair course with multiple water features that come into play throughout the course. This Par 71, tree-lined course is ideal for individual play, group golf tournaments and outings.

The Golf Club at Ballantyne is among the finest PGA public golf courses in the region. Passionate about providing a destination golf experience, The Golf Club has continued to receive numerous accolades since inception including 4.5 Stars by Golf Digest and named as a top 50 Golf Resort by Golf World.
