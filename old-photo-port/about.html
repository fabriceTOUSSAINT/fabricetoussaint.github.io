Fabrice's a photographer who dreams of clean lines and symmetry.


Residing on the east coast city of Philadelphia, Fabrice is constantly inspired from his good friends, good food and beautiful architecture. When Fabrice is not in front of the computer you'll find him out skateboarding around the city, exploring the questionable, hanging out with friends and overall sticking his nose into the unknown to make it known. With his camera around his shoulder as often as his head is, Fabrice values every moment in life as its own, and every moment deserves to be remembered.
