HARDWOOD LEGENDS DINNERS

These exclusive experiences are hosted by HoopTee CGC charity founder, Fred Whitfield. An evening full of connecting business's and sports executives with several hardwood personalities. Proceeds support HoopTee’s mission of providing at risk youth opportunities to participate in sports camps and educational programs they cannot afford and would not otherwise be able to participate.

WE ARE HOOPTEE

The HoopTee Celebrity Golf Classic founded by Fred Whitfield, is the primary fundraising event for HoopTee Charities, Inc., a N.C. Non-Profit Corporation, that supports youth-based charitable activities in the Carolinas.

Help us raise funds to continue our mission of positively impacting disadvantaged youth through sports and educational programs. We appreciate your support!
