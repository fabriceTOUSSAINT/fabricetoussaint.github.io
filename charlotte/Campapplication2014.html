The purpose of the Achievements Unlimited Basketball School, LLC is to provide basic fundamental basketball instruction to every camper. The instruction will be geared to cover all aspects of the game and will be tailored to the different age levels of campers. Additionally, in our efforts to help mold well-rounded future student-athletes, we stress the important of academics and the need for a strong stance against DRUG ABUSE through lectures from successful student-athlete role models from the NBA, ACC & NFL. The fee for each camper is $285.00 and includes: Fundamental Instruction, Supplemental Insurance, Daily Lunch, Progress Report, Team Picture, Anti-Drug Education, Camp T-Shirt, Gift Bags, Lectures by NBA, NFL athletes as well as other sports celebrities and Awards for tournament and contests winners.
